How it works

Discover. Prioritize. Prove.

One scanner takes you from "we don't know what crypto we run" to an auditor-ready bill of materials — without sending a line of code anywhere.

1 Discover

Every language, every repo

Scans source and Git repositories for quantum-vulnerable algorithms — RSA, ECC, DSA, Diffie-Hellman, legacy hashes and key material — across 15+ languages and your IaC.

2 Prioritize

Risk, with honest confidence

Each finding gets a migration risk score and a confidence level. Real call-sites and key material are flagged as exposure; bare names in logs, configs and comments are separated as possible mentions — so the headline number is one you can trust.

3 Prove

The CBOM regulators ask for

Generates a standards CycloneDX Cryptography Bill of Materials plus control-gap reports mapped to CNSA 2.0, NIST CSF, FISMA and more — the evidence your auditors and customers want.

On-prem by design

Your source never leaves your network.

Crypto discovery means reading your most sensitive code. So UniQueS is built to run entirely inside your boundary — a single self-hostable binary with no external dependencies, no phone-home, and no cloud upload. The same architecture that makes it air-gap-friendly is the reason you can trust it with the source.
